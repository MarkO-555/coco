ASCII & text files information                    
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Files in this area are a mixture of straight ASCII, RSDOS & OS9 archives.
RSDOS archives (.ARC & .ARK) can be unarchived with TC40 & EZARC respectively.
OS9 archives (.PAK, .AR, .LZH, .ZIP, .ARJ, .ARC) can be unarchived with their
respective UNarchivers.

All the archive/unarchive utilities are available on this BBS.

As you can see .ARC is used in both OS9 and RSDOS archives, the 2 types are
NOT compatable but there will be a note in the OS9 file description.
